CS2 Round Phases

This site explains how Counter-Strike 2 rounds change from early to mid to late game. I made this because I used to struggle with mid-round decisions and economy management.

Overview

  • Early game (Rounds 1–3): pistol rounds and early economy.
  • Mid game (Rounds 4–10): full buys, utility, and map control.
  • Late game (Rounds 11+): executes, clutches, and saving weapons.

General rule: your buy should match your plan for the round.
