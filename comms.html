Simple comm rules

Early rounds

  • Call first contact fast.
  • Say number + location (“2 mid”).
  • Don’t over explain while fighting.

Example: “2 mid, one lit, falling back.”

Mid rounds

  • Share what’s cleared (“A main clear”).
  • Call utility timing (“smoke in 3…2…1”).
  • Don’t rotate just because you heard noise once.

Late / clutch

  • Dead players: give one useful piece of info, then stop talking.
  • Alive player: focus on sound and time.
  • Don’t backseat the clutch.

Roles (basic)

Not official, just how I think about it:

IGL: calls buys and the plan.
Entry: goes first and creates space.
Support: flashes and trades.
Lurker: watches rotations and timings.
Anchor: holds a site and stays alive.

Practice ideas

  • Play one session focused only on trading (stay close enough to trade).
  • Practice one smoke lineup per day (don’t try to learn 50 at once).
  • Sound discipline: mix walking with Shift and quick timing bursts.
